📌 5 Things Every Pune Broker Must Know Today
  • Demand-Supply Gap Driving Prices: 2025 saw 81,000 units sold against only 64,000 launched — a 17,000-unit absorption surplus that is actively pushing prices upward across all zones.
  • EMIs at 4-Year Low — Push Fence-Sitters NOW: RBI repo at 5.25%. HDFC from 7.20%, SBI from 7.25%. EMI on ₹60L (20yr) dropped from ~₹53K (2023) to ~₹46K today — ₹84,000 saved per year.
  • Mahalunge Is the Fastest Appreciating Zone: 10–14% YoY — higher than Baner, Kharadi, or Hinjewadi. Still priced at ₹9,000–13,000/sqft vs Baner at ₹16,000+. Early entry window open now.
  • Metro Line 3 (94% Done) — Imminent Inauguration: Hinjewadi–Shivajinagar corridor near opening. Every metro corridor in India surged 15–20% post-inauguration. Buy near stations before it opens.
  • Only Recommend RERA-Compliant Projects: MahaRERA suspended 1,905+ projects state-wide in a major compliance sweep (with Pune leading the breakdown). QR codes are now mandatory on all ads. Scan before every client meeting at maharera.mahaonline.gov.in.

🏙️ TOD (Transit-Oriented Development) Zones — What Brokers Need to Know
  • Higher FSI Within 500m of Metro Stations: PMC's DP 2041 grants higher Floor Space Index (FSI) and permits mixed-use development in TOD zones. More buildable space = future supply surge in these areas.
  • 10–25% Annual Appreciation Near Metro: Properties within 500m–1km of operational metro stations are showing 10–25% YoY appreciation — outperforming the city average of 5–12%.
  • UDCPR Extended to Full PMRDA: Unified Development Control and Promotion Regulations now apply across the entire Pune Metropolitan Region — standardizes approvals and development norms city-wide.
  • Broker Play: Near-ready inventory in TOD zones (Aundh, Balewadi, Kharadi, Hadapsar) offers best current value before the post-inauguration premium and post-approval supply surge.
